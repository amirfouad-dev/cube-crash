## Full description (4000 chars max)
```
⚡ CRASH THE CUBES. CHAIN THE SURGE. ⚡

Cube Crash is a neon-cyberpunk block puzzle: drag energy cubes onto the 8×8
power grid, complete rows and columns to crash them in a blaze of light, and
chain clears to push your combo multiplier to the limit.

🎮 SIMPLE TO PLAY, HARD TO PUT DOWN
• Drag the active piece onto the grid — the NEXT window shows what's coming
• Tap the piece to rotate it — four directions
• Fill a full row or column to crash it
• Clear again within 3 moves to keep your combo alive (up to ×3 score!)

⏱ THREE WAYS TO PLAY
• BEGINNER — classic and untimed. Just you and the grid.
• INTERMEDIATE — 30 seconds to place each piece. Think fast.
• ADVANCED — the clock shrinks by 1 second every 2,000 points. How deep
  can you go before the grid outruns you?

🔥 BUILT TO FEEL AMAZING
• Combo heat meter that charges as you chain — watch it drain as your
  streak nears death
• Screen shake, particle bursts and slow-mo on big multi-line crashes
• A synthwave soundtrack and a melodic crash sound that climbs the scale
  with your combo
• Haptic feedback tuned to every placement and surge

🏆 ALWAYS FAIR
Cube Crash's piece generator mathematically guarantees every piece it deals
can be placed after the one before it. If you lose, it was the plan — not
the deck. No pay-to-win, no artificial difficulty spikes.

💠 EARN, UNLOCK, RETURN
• 3 fresh missions every day
• Daily streak rewards — keep the grid lit
• 8 unlockable visual themes: Vaporwave, Matrix, Hologram, Sunset Drive,
  Reactor Core, Deep Space, Ghost Protocol… all earned by playing

🔒 TRULY FREE. TRULY PRIVATE.
• No ads. No in-app purchases. No account.
• Works 100% offline — the app doesn't even have internet permission
• No data collection of any kind

One more surge. One more combo. One more second on the clock.
BOOT THE GRID.
```
