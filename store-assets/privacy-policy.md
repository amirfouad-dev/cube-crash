# Cube Crash — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 3, 2026_

Cube Crash is a free offline puzzle game.

## Data we collect

**None.**

- Cube Crash has **no internet access** (the app does not request the INTERNET
  permission, so it is technically incapable of sending data anywhere).
- No analytics, no advertising SDKs, no tracking, no accounts.
- Your scores, streaks, missions, and settings are stored **only on your
  device** and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Permissions

- **Vibration** — used for gameplay haptic feedback only. Can be turned off in
  Settings.

## Children

Cube Crash is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone.
